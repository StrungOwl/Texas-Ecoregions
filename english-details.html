Chihuahuan Deserts

Invasive Species

The Chihuahuan Desert is a warm desert located in southwestern North America. It is the largest hot desert in North America, with a landscape featuring arid mountains, desert basins, and sparse vegetation adapted to extreme heat and drought conditions.

Invasive Species

Arizona/New Mexico Mountains

Invasive Species

The Arizona/New Mexico Mountains ecoregion is a mountain region characterized by coniferous forests and rugged terrain at high elevations. This area features ponderosa pine, spruce, and fir forests adapted to cooler temperatures and higher precipitation, with diverse understory vegetation and endemic plant species unique to the southwestern mountain ecosystem.
